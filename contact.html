Getting Help

To those who are suffering with depression, anxiety and low self-esteem I understand the dysfunction this brings into your life. It can affect anybody and is so often misunderstood

Individual Therapy For adults & children Break through the subconscious patterns of thought that are holding you back We are living in challenging times that demand a lot from us. This pressure can trigger our past hurts to come to the surface complicating life even further. At the same time, we are privileged to live in these times because it offers a golden opportunity for personal growth. Every challenge contains a hidden gift. More than ever before people are realizing the importance of emotional health and are making it a priority in their lives. I offer advanced forms of therapy that make it possible to identity and address the root cause of problems, leading to effective results. You don’t have to be a victim of life, Rise to the occasion. See life as an adventure to be lived, not as a crisis to be handled.





Marriage Counselling

Are you frustrated with repeating the same old style of communication, blaming, criticizing, belittling, judging each other? You might think at times that you married the wrong person, that you’re completely incompatible. Imago therapy can open a whole new world of understanding and hope to you. You will learn why you were so strongly attracted to each other and what caused that love to seem to evaporate. Imago therapy provides tools to foster personal growth, mutual understanding and happiness. I invite you to invest in your marriage and turn it into your biggest asset and joy in life.

I believe in the healing power of human connection and empathetic understanding without judgement. I strive to create a safe and secure environment for every client to feel safe while expressing their emotions. The client and I work together as a team and I take into account their unique concerns/situation in order to achieve the best outcomes.
